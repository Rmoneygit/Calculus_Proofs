	\textbf{Definition:} Let $f$ be a real valued function. We call $f$ a \textbf{rational function} if
	$$f(x) = \frac{p(x)}{q(x)}$$
	where $p(x)$ and $q(x)$ are polynomials and $q$ is not the zero function.
